V-spec II Nür

The pinnacle of R34 performance, the V-Spec II Nür is a rare beast only 718 built, each infused with racing DNA. Named after the Nürburgring, it features an N1-based RB26DETT, stiffer suspension, carbon fiber touches, and a 300 km/h speedometer. It’s more than a Skyline it’s the final evolution.

The ATTESSA ET-S

Nissan’s ATTESA E-TS (Advanced Total Traction Engineering System for All-Terrain with Electronic Torque Split) all-wheel-drive system is the secret weapon behind the R34 Skyline GT-R’s legendary grip and dynamic prowess. Engineered for precision, this sophisticated AWD system constantly monitors inputs like throttle position, wheel speed, steering angle, and lateral G-forces in real time. It then instantly adjusts torque distribution between the front and rear axles sending power where it's needed most to maintain maximum traction.

The Legendary RB26DETT

Born for performance, the RB26DETT is a 2.6L twin-turbocharged inline-six that powers the legendary Nissan Skyline GT-R R34 — a car that earned the nickname “Godzilla” for good reason. Engineered as a race-bred powerplant, the RB26DETT features a robust cast-iron block, forged steel crankshaft, and high-flow DOHC 24-valve aluminum head, designed to handle serious boost and high RPMs straight from the factory. With a redline of 8,000 rpm and a silky-smooth powerband, it's built to scream.
